My Work / My Projects
Breaking
In this short film, I introduce different time points leading to present through an intense montage of footage captured at different places.
Dance
In this video, I am mainly focusing on the vibe change when doing something you love. I show it using different color gradings and angles.
The Perception
The short film i made using drone and iphone cameras to illustrate difference in proximity as in relation to how differently we can percieve things.
BMW Reel
